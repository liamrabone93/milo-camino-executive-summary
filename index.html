What Resonated
Four things that separate Milo from Tenzo.

Familiarity

A tool the team already knows how to use

Milo works the way Claude and ChatGPT do — you ask a question in plain English and get an answer back. Your teams are already using tools like this day to day, so there's no dashboard training curve and onboarding is quick.

Coverage

Marketing analysis well beyond Tenzo

Milo reads Meta, Instagram, TikTok and YouTube alongside your trading data. This is the clearest gap in the current setup and the strongest single reason for the marketing team to want it.

Distribution

Automations that go to the people who need them

Scheduled reports and alerts pushed automatically to GMs, owners and investors over Slack, WhatsApp and email. Nobody has to log in, build a report, or chase anyone for numbers.

Assurance

Security and data handling, documented

Trust and security documentation covering how Camino's data would be stored, accessed and protected — read-only connections, role-based access, and the certifications behind it.

The Biggest Gap
Marketing data — where the current setup runs out.

The problem today

Tenzo is built around operational and labour data — sales, covers, scheduling, forecasting. It does that job, but it was never designed to read your marketing channels.

So marketing sits outside the reporting stack entirely: someone exports from Meta, someone else checks Instagram and TikTok natively, YouTube is looked at separately, and none of it lines up against trading data without manual work in a spreadsheet. The question "what did that campaign actually do to covers?" takes a person a morning to answer, if it gets answered at all.

✓Meta & Instagram

Paid and organic in one place — spend, reach, engagement and return, split by site or campaign.

✓TikTok

Content performance and ad spend, reported the same way the platform reports it natively.

✓YouTube

Views, retention and audience data pulled in alongside everything else, not checked separately.

✓Next to your trading data

Marketing sits beside sales and covers, so spend can be read against what it actually delivered.
